ABOUT US

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognised as one of the world's premier luxury locations and to provide personal experiences for our visitors that they will remember for a lifetime. The needs of our guests, associates and owners are in the forefront of everything we do.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest level of hospitality to meet the needs and expectations of our guests. Our goal is to make AMRO Hotels, a place for pleasant stay, fun meetings and gala ceremonies.

Our Values

Integrity is the foundation for everything we do. We are admired and respected for our commitment to honesty, trust, and transparency.

Board of Directors

The Board of Directors is the driving force of the organization.The purpose of the board of directors includes formulating organizational policies, formulating strategies to achieve goals, ensuring that operations comply with relevant laws and regulations, and ensuring that any decision or action is in the interests of all stakeholders.
The board of directors represents and protects the interests of shareholders or individuals, companies or institutions that own at least one share of the company. They also help to make important decisions about any issues the company or organization may face.
